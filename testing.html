The History of Jokes

According to the British Museum, the first recorded joke dates to around 2600 BCE. Authored on the Westcar Papyrus by Pharaoh Snefru's court magician, Djadjamankh, it reads: "How do you entertain a bored pharaoh? You sail a boatload of young women dressed only in fishing nets down the Nile and urge the pharaoh to go catch a fish."
